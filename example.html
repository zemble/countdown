Airport Board Countdown Timer - Examples

This page demonstrates various configurations of the Airport Board Countdown Timer for embedding in websites and Moodle courses.

Example 1: Basic Countdown (Next New Year)

Default countdown to next New Year's Day at midnight UTC.

<iframe src="https://yourusername.github.io/countdown/" width="800" height="300" frameborder="0"></iframe>

Example 2: Custom Date and Title

Countdown to a specific date with custom title.

<iframe src="https://yourusername.github.io/countdown/?date=2024-12-25&time=00:00:00&title=Christmas%20Day%20Countdown" width="800" height="300" frameborder="0"></iframe>

Example 3: Course Deadline with Timezone

Perfect for Moodle integration - shows a course deadline in Eastern Time.

<iframe src="https://yourusername.github.io/countdown/?date=2024-05-15&time=23:59:00&timezone=America/New_York&title=Final%20Project%20Due" width="800" height="300" frameborder="0"></iframe>

Example 4: Responsive Mobile View

Smaller size perfect for mobile devices or sidebar widgets.

<iframe src="https://yourusername.github.io/countdown/?date=2024-07-04&time=18:00:00&timezone=America/Los_Angeles&title=Independence%20Day" width="400" height="350" frameborder="0"></iframe>

frameborder="0"

</iframe>
</div>

URL Parameter Reference

Available Parameters:
• date=YYYY-MM-DD (target date)
• time=HH:MM:SS (target time, 24-hour format)
• timezone=TIMEZONE (e.g., America/New_York, Europe/London)
• title=YOUR_TITLE (URL encoded spaces as %20)
• theme=THEME_NAME (currently: default)

Example URL:
https://yourusername.github.io/countdown/?date=2024-06-15&time=14:30:00&timezone=America/Chicago&title=Summer%20Event

Common Timezones

US Timezones:
• America/New_York (Eastern)
• America/Chicago (Central)
• America/Denver (Mountain)
• America/Los_Angeles (Pacific)

International:
• Europe/London (GMT/BST)
• Europe/Paris (CET/CEST)
• Asia/Tokyo (JST)
• Australia/Sydney (AEST/AEDT)
• UTC (Coordinated Universal Time)
